🏠 Overview

Landlord USDT is a play-to-earn DeFi yield game built on BNB Chain. Players deposit USDT to receive Gold, purchase virtual properties that generate daily USDT yield, and grow their empire through compounding and strategic boost purchases.

The game runs entirely on a smart contract — all deposits, yields, purchases, and withdrawals are executed on-chain with full transparency.

Core Loop: Deposit USDT → Get Gold → Buy Properties → Earn Daily Yield → Compound or Withdraw

🚀 Getting Started

1
Connect Your Wallet

Use MetaMask or any Web3-compatible wallet. The app will automatically prompt you to switch to BNB Chain if needed.

2
Deposit USDT

Enter the amount of USDT you want to deposit. You'll need to approve the USDT spend first (one-time). You receive Gold at a 1:1 ratio with zero deposit fees.

3
Buy Properties

Use your Gold to purchase properties. Each property generates a different daily yield rate. You can own multiple copies of each property.

4
Earn & Grow

Your properties generate USDT yield every second. Claim free Gold every 4 hours. Compound your earnings for a +10% Gold bonus, or withdraw your earned USDT.

🪙 Gold System

Gold is the in-game currency used to purchase properties. It is obtained by:

• Depositing USDT — 1 USDT = 1 Gold (no fee)
• Compounding earnings — Convert earned USDT back to Gold with a +10% bonus (2.5% fee)
• Free claims — Claim 0.1-0.2% of your total deposits as free Gold every 4 hours
⚠️ Gold is subject to a 1% daily tax that is applied continuously. Invest your Gold into properties quickly to minimize tax loss.

📈 Yield & Income

Your properties generate USDT yield continuously based on time elapsed. The yield formula is:

Daily USDT = Σ (Building Cost × Daily Rate × Quantity)
If boosts active: Daily USDT × (1 + Boost %)

Example: Owning 5× Coffee Shops ($25, 0.07%/day) earns you:

25 × 0.0007 × 5 = 0.0875 USDT/day

Yield accumulates as "Earned USDT" which can be compounded or withdrawn at any time.

🎁 Free Gold Claim

Every 4 hours, you can claim free Gold worth 0.1% to 0.2% of your total deposited principal. The exact percentage varies each claim using on-chain randomness.

Example: If you deposited $100 total, each free claim gives you 0.10 to 0.20 Gold. That's up to 1.2 Gold per day for free!

The timer resets after each claim. You must have at least one deposit to be eligible for free claims.

🔄 Compounding

Compounding converts your earned USDT back into Gold so you can buy more properties. Here's how it works:

+ All earned USDT is converted to Gold
− 2.5% compound fee is deducted (sent to protocol)
+ You receive a 10% bonus on the compounded amount
Gold Received = (Earnings − 2.5% fee) × 1.10
Example: $10 earned → $9.75 after fee → 10.725 Gold received

💸 Withdrawals

You can withdraw your earned USDT at any time. Important rules:

🔒 Your deposited principal (initial USDT) is permanently locked and cannot be withdrawn. Only earned yield (USDT from property income) can be withdrawn.

A 10% withdrawal fee applies to every withdrawal. The fee is sent to the protocol owner's wallet.

You receive = Withdrawal Amount × 90%
Example: Withdraw $10 → You get $9, $1 fee

💀 Daily Tax

A 1% daily tax is applied to your Gold balance continuously. This tax is prorated per second — meaning the tax accumulates smoothly over time, not in big daily chunks.

Strategy tip: Convert your Gold into properties as quickly as possible. Properties are not subject to the Gold tax — only unspent Gold sitting in your balance is taxed.

❓ FAQ

Q: Can I withdraw my initial deposit?
No. All deposited USDT (principal) is permanently locked. Only earned USDT from property yield can be withdrawn.
Q: How often is yield paid?
Yield accrues every second in real-time. You can collect, compound, or withdraw it at any time.
Q: Can I sell my properties?
Currently, properties cannot be sold. Once purchased, they permanently generate yield for your account.
Q: What happens if I don't claim my free Gold?
Nothing — free claims don't expire. The 4-hour cooldown simply prevents claiming more than 6 times per day. Unclaimed rewards are not lost.
Q: Can I stack multiple boosts?
Yes! Multiple boosts stack additively. For example, Caffeine Rush (+2%) and Happy Hour (+5%) together give +7% yield.
Q: Which chain does this run on?
Landlord USDT runs on BNB Smart Chain (BSC). You need BNB for gas fees and USDT (BEP-20) to play.
