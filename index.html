Overview

Landlord USDT is a play-to-earn DeFi game on BNB Chain. Deposit USDT to get Gold (1:1), buy properties that earn daily USDT yield, then withdraw or convert earnings to Gold with a 15% bonus.

Getting Started

1. Connect MetaMask (BNB Chain). 2. Deposit USDT — get Gold 1:1. 3. Buy properties with Gold. 4. Earn USDT yield every second. 5. Activate free 5% boost every 4 hours. 6. Withdraw USDT or convert to Gold (+15% bonus).

Building Price Scaling

The first 10 of any building type cost the base price. From the 11th purchase onward, each building costs 1% more than the previous one (compounding). This scaling stops at the 50th building — from #51 onward the price stays flat at the 50th building's price.

Yield

Properties generate USDT yield every second. Formula: Building Cost × Daily Rate × Quantity. Boosts multiply total yield additively. The Lemonade Stand has the highest yield rate relative to cost.

Free 5% Yield Boost

Every 4 hours you can activate a free +5% yield boost that lasts 4 hours. You must own at least one building to use this. The boost stacks with paid boosts. On mobile, the boost button appears at the top of the game area.

Convert / Withdraw

Convert to Gold: Turns earned USDT into Gold. 2.5% fee to the owner, but you receive a +15% Gold bonus on the converted amount. Withdraw: Cash out earned USDT with 10% fee. Deposited principal is permanently locked.

🔍 Smart Contract

Landlord USDT runs entirely on a verified BNB Chain smart contract. All deposits, yields, purchases, and withdrawals are executed on-chain with full transparency. There is no emergency withdraw or admin yield override — once deployed, building yields are permanent and funds cannot be drained by the owner.

Safety: No emergencyWithdraw function exists. No updateBuildingYield function exists. The owner cannot change yields or drain the contract. Only setTreasury, addBuilding, addBoost, and transferOwnership are available to the owner.

❓ FAQ

Can I withdraw my initial deposit?
No. All deposited USDT (principal) is permanently locked in the contract. Only earned USDT from property yield can be withdrawn.
Can the developer change building yields or drain the contract?
No. The smart contract has no emergencyWithdraw or updateBuildingYield function. Once deployed, yields are fixed and funds cannot be removed by anyone except through normal gameplay withdrawals.
Is this a ponzi? What is the treasury for?
No. Typically these style games are ponzi based that rely on only new users coming in, however we will be building actual yield farms and all profits go directly to the TVL throughout the game (Each deposit will be publicly announced). In an ideal world we will look to over collateralise the TVL!
Why do building prices increase?
The first 10 of any building type cost the base price. From the 11th onward, each costs 1% more (compounding). This scaling caps at the 50th building — from #51 onward the price stays flat. This prevents whales from dominating cheaply.
Who is the developer?
Landlord USDT was developed by Kader . The smart contract is open-source and verified on BscScan. Kader has been in the defi/web3 space for over 5 years, he also owns a decentralised exchange and highly popular memecoin launchpad with over 50,000 tokens launched on it to date.
