Overview

Landlord USDT is a play-to-earn DeFi game on BNB Chain. Deposit USDT to get Gold (1:1), buy properties that generate daily USDT yield, then withdraw or convert earnings back to Gold with a 10% bonus.

Getting Started

1. Connect your MetaMask wallet (BNB Chain). 2. Deposit USDT — you receive Gold at 1:1. 3. Buy properties with Gold. 4. Earn USDT yield every second. 5. Withdraw USDT or convert to Gold (+10% bonus).

Properties

10 properties available, priced in Gold from 0.10 to 10.00. Each generates a different daily USDT yield rate. When you buy a building, 90% of the Gold value stays in the TVL contract and 10% goes to the treasury wallet.

Yield

Properties generate USDT yield every second. Formula: Building Cost × Daily Rate × Quantity. Boosts multiply total yield additively.

Convert / Withdraw

Convert to Gold: Turns earned USDT into Gold with a 2.5% fee but +10% Gold bonus. Withdraw: Cash out earned USDT with a 10% fee. Your deposited principal is permanently locked.

Referrals

Share your referral link. When someone deposits using your link, you earn 10% of their deposit as bonus Gold. Referrer is locked on first deposit and cannot be changed.

Boosts

Buy temporary yield boosts with USDT. They increase your total yield output for a limited time. Multiple boosts stack.
